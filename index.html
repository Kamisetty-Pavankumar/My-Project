MY Favourite Films (Movies)

1st Film Coolie

Coolie (2025), directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, stars Rajinikanth as Deva, a former coolie-union leader who gets pulled back into a world of crime and secrets after the mysterious death of his friend. As he investigates, he faces a powerful smuggling syndicate and uncovers shocking truths tied to his own past. Along the way, he protects Preethi (Shruti Haasan) and rekindles old alliances, leading to intense battles and emotional revelations. With high-octane action, stylish visuals, and music by Anirudh Ravichander, the film blends revenge, loyalty, and redemption. Released in August 2025, it became one of the year’s biggest Tamil blockbusters.

2nd Flim HIT-3 Case

Hit: The Third Case (HIT 3) is the next installment in the popular Telugu crime thriller franchise. The movie continues with the Homicide Intervention Team (HIT), diving deep into another mysterious case filled with suspense, twists, and shocking revelations. It focuses on solving a complex crime while dealing with the personal struggles of the lead investigator. Like its previous parts, the film blends intense investigation, psychological tension, and emotional depth. With gripping storytelling and unpredictable turns, HIT 3 keeps the audience hooked till the climax. It is one of the most awaited thrillers for fans of crime and suspense dramas.

3rd Film Kingdom

Kingdom (2025) is a Telugu action drama starring Vijay Deverakonda, directed by Gowtam Tinnanuri. Vijay plays Suri, a hot-headed police constable haunted by his brother’s disappearance. He goes undercover 
        in Sri Lanka, hoping to find him while unraveling a dangerous mission. The film blends emotional family drama with action and a tribal lore subplot set in the 1920s. Music by Anirudh Ravichander and rich 
        visuals elevate the experience. Released on 31 July 2025, it later streamed on Netflix from 27 August 2025.
